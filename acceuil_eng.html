Prepare your itinerary

Preparing an itinerary is essential to making the most of Kyoto. With its many monuments and must-see sites, careful planning will save you time and ensure you don't miss a thing!

Rules of etiquette at Kyoto

Once you have arrived in Kyoto, they may look askance at you because of tatoos or behavior that may upset the locals. So before heading to Kyoto, make sure you know the rules of etiquette.
